# Supply Chain Analytics – Insight Summary

## Overview
This analysis explores supply chain performance using the DataCo Global Supply Chain dataset. The objective is to identify operational inefficiencies and business opportunities.

## Key Insights

### 1. Regional Sales Performance
Western Europe and Southeast Asia generate the highest sales revenue.

### 2. Customer Segment Contribution
The Consumer segment contributes the largest share of orders and revenue.

### 3. Shipping Performance
Standard Class shipping accounts for most deliveries but has the highest average delivery delay.

### 4. Profit Distribution
Certain product categories show high sales but low profit margins.

### 5. Operational Risk
Regions with high shipping delays indicate potential logistics bottlenecks.

## Business Recommendations

- Improve logistics efficiency in high-delay regions.
- Promote high-profit product categories.
- Optimize shipping modes for faster delivery.
